Fishermen’s Delights VR Installation


VR Experience / Installation


Overview

"Fishermen’s Delights VR Installation" is a virtual reality game demonstrating the fishing entertainment in the Northern Sung Dynasty. It builds bridges between the collections of National Palace Museum and people.


Role

Visual Designer, Unity Development

Unity, Photoshop, Illustrator

Febuary 2018 - July 2018



Background

The scene, Jin Ming Pool (金明池), is at theoutside of Fu Shun Tian Men (府順天門) and it was at the opposite side of a royal garden. Jin Ming Pool was a place where the emperor feasted on. In every March and April, the Jin Ming Pool was opened to the public. This big event was described as a saying: the crowd of fishers is like swarming ants and the public is like a wall. At that time, when fishermen caught fish, they bought the fish and made it a delectable traditional Chinese dish by asking a vendor to manage cooking.


Design Process


Concept

The game is based on the ancient paintings "Touring the Imperial Gardens in Spring, Chao Po-Chu(趙伯駒)" and “Fishermen’s Delights” from the National Palace Museum. The prosperity of Jin Ming Pool is reproduced by visual reality in order to bring players back to Sung dynasty.



Details

Players can enjoy the seamless dynamic patterns of fun fishing and the fishing rod as the feedback of weight, vibrations, and directions is given to the players. In the game, players will receive a delicious dish when they success. Fishing in Jin Ming Pool is able to let players experience Chinese old times and to give them the joyfulness of fishing.



VR Experience

The game begins with an introduction of the story. With a brief animation, the player explores the environment of Jin Ming pool and the background of Sung dynasty.


When the player is guided to pick up the fishing rod and then see the buoy falling into water, fishing begins.
The player decides that whether the fish is hooked by observing the floating of buoy and the variation of water surface. To gather more fish, the player needs to be calm and constantly concentrate.



As the fish is on the hook, the player immediately turns the handle grip clockwise to reel back in. It is the stage to pull the fish. The swimming direction and distance of fish gives the player corresponding swing feedback.



When the player takes a line for a while, the fish will be caught and cooked into a delectable dish. The games allows the player to experience that how people in Sung dynasty go fishing in Jin Ming Pool. The ending shows the hospitality of serving the fish dish.
