Greens


Installation


Overview

Greens is an interactive device portraying a sci-fi and technology-laden ecosystem. It discusses the interdependent, inter-oppressed but intertwined relationship among humans, nature, and technology. Via extracting mutable features from the environment and transferring user’s voice data, Greens creates a system that makes invisible data from our environment visible and makes society aware of the impact of nature.


Role

Interaction Designer

Hardware Fabrication, VUI Design, Prototyping

July 2019 - November 2019



Background

Over the last few decades, the exploitation of our planet and the degradation of our environment has gone up at an alarming rate. Our living planet Earth is currently facing a lot of environmental concerns. The environmental problems like global warming, acid rain, air pollution, urban sprawl, waste disposal, ozone layer depletion, water pollution, climate change and many more affect every human, animal, and nation on this planet.


Design Process


Concept

In our lives, there are all kinds of environmental data, which are important indicators and messages in the ecosystem. However, these data are invisible, so it is difficult for people to perceive its existence and changes, and ignore that our daily choices are closely related to the drastic changes in the environment. Human behaviors may sometimes inevitably change the surrounding environment, and moreover, would cause different degree of problems.
How might we effectively use this data and make social impact ? Let people see, feel, or hear these intangible data more deeply and further raise environmental awareness.



Details

We designed an interactive device — Greens with environmentally friendly and recyclable materials as the main material. The core of this interactive device includes: voice user interface, tangible user interface, and experience design.



Greens provide a process of fluid experience by serial voice inputs, entailing the outputs of jazzy AI music, sci-fi projecting images, and futuristic mechanical ecosystem model.



Implementation

The installation mainly uses recyclable materials and uses laser cutting to complete most of the assembly components. The design style is based on technology, geometry, and abstract appearance.



Voice User Interface

We use Google Assistant to build our voice-based interface. Google Assistant SDK is set up on the dev board, and the voice interface is built with Dialogflow with testing in Action On Google.



Shape Changing TUI

We design a few paper models and paper sculptures, they represent different roles in the sci-fi ecosystem. Arduino control system would receive the voice inputs and activate different motors in our mechanical device, which contains a lot of interactive elements of different shapes. These objects are composed of different paper materials and cardboard-based mechanism interfaces.




Music Generation & Mapping

Users’ voice inputs enable the Coral TPU board to trigger the pygame module to play the auto-generated jazz music produced by Tensorflow and pymidi module, and also would change our projecting images controlled by Madmapper.



Demostration

The interactive dialogue is divided into four rounds. The voice interface is used to record people's carbon footprint, and the data is converted into automatically generated music and tangible objects. Through interactive installation - Greens, it is expected that the designed experience can arouse people's awareness of environmental protection.
Let's go green together!
