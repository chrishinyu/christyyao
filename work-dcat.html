Nctu Dcat Web Design


Web Design


Overview

This is a web design project. I redesigned the official website of the Communication Technology Department of NCTU. By understanding the teaching philosophy and characteristics that the department wants to show, reconstruct the interactive map and information architecture from the old website structure, and convey the personality and style of the department with a brand new visual appearance.


Role

Designer

Illustrator, XD, After Effects, Photoshop

March 2018 - September 2019


Background

In the Department of Communication and Technology, NCTU, information and communication technology continue to innovate, which not only brings digital convergence but also blurs the boundaries between traditional media. As the only Department of Communication and Science and Technology in Taiwan, it emphasizes the integration of communication majors, interdisciplinary, and multi-skill learning.

Design Process

Concept

Using bright and vivid main colors, overlapping geometric figures echo each other, fusing different textures and textures, and presenting gradation effects, trying to visually show the concept of this department-the combination of "humanities, technology, and creativity".


Details

The whole website uses the main visual element and design to create motion graphics showing the different themes.

Mobile UI

Responsive web design (RWD) makes web pages render well on a variety of devices and window or screen sizes.
