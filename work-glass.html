Glassmorphism Wallet


UI Design


Overview

It's a redesign work of iphone app - Wallet. Trying to design the latest UI trends to show a new style of "Glassmorphism Wallet".


Role

UI Designer

Figma, Illustrator

December 2020



Concept

GLASSMORPHISM is an upcoming UI trend and it’s growing in popularity. This UI element is designed to resemble the feature of glass. The design idea is to blur the background of the element to give a transparent glassy look.



I tried to redesign the application - Wallet, and I redesign it in another style, showing a clear, glassy, icy and snowy appearance.



Details

The design characteristics are: monotone color style in two mode, transparency and frosted-glass effect, a subtle border on the translucent objects.
